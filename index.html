Hi,

I am a web developer and generally an artistic person. I like letter illustration, drawing and photography. I believe that being a frontend web developer is the perfect combination between art and technology, which makes me love what I do.


I’ve gotten my hands on some backend work as well. I like to have the ability to take a website further than frontend work and be able to make powerful websites. At my previous job I’ve worked with CakePhp. In my free time, I’ve built light CRUD systems with other MVC frameworks like Ruby On Rails and Laravel.


In the future I want to reach a mix between illustrator and frontend developer that goes beyond just aesthetics. I want to be able to create websites that display a look and feel along with the message the client wants to deliver.
